Test Minimum Consumption with Defaults, Vary Borrow Bound

As Borrowing bound relaxes, more borrowing. As borrowing bound is relaxed, the fraction of people defaulting is increasing. pYisMINY captures fraction of households defaulting. Note that this fraction was close to almost exactly zero when default was not allowed due to the natural borrowing constraint.

Much greater consumption variance, is this calculated correctly?, when there is relaxed borrowing constraint. Consumption is not calculated correctly for borrowing.
